David Foust

Before he played Quarterback at University of Maryland College Park, Dave tallied 2781 passing yards for 34 touchdowns with a 63% completion percentage at South River High School. He won the Rhodes Trophy for Anne Arundel County Most Valuable Player and was 2019 Capital Gazette Football Player of the Year.
